# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want participation in this project to be safe, respectful, and productive. Contributors must:

- communicate with empathy and assume good intent;
- discuss technical ideas without attacking people;
- respect privacy, consent, platform rules, and intellectual-property rights;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, and disclosure of private data;
- accept maintainer decisions about project scope and safety boundaries.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other public or
private spaces used to represent the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the same channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
Do not include sensitive personal information in a public issue. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, reject contributions, temporarily limit participation, or permanently ban participants when
necessary to protect the community.

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a reporter or witness is
not allowed.
